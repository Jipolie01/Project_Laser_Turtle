Play function.

Play sound by given parameters frequency, duration, amount, amount_wait. Frequency is used in a formule to math half period(time between speaker on and speaker off). Duration is the amount of systemtime the sound is ale to play. Amount is how many times the sound will play. Amount_wait is the time between everytime the sound is playing. This function dus not return anything.
